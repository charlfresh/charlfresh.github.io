Sweet Potatoes With Marshmallows

Jump to recipe

Background

The Recipe

Ingredients
peeled and sliced sweet potatoes 5
packed brown sugar 1/2 cup
butter 1/4 cup
orange juice 3 tbsp
ground cinnamon one pinch
miniature marshmallows 1(10.5 oz) package
Intructions
step 1 Gather all ingredients, and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
step 2 Place sweet potatoes in a large saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil and cook until tender, about 15 minutes.
step 3 Remove from heat, drain, and then mash
step 4 Place mashed sweet potatoes in a large bowl. Add brown sugar, butter, orange juice, and cinnamon; mix with an electric mixer until blended.
step 5 Spread evenly into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Sprinkle marshmallows over top.
step 6 Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is heated through and marshmallows are puffed and golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
step 7 Serve and enjoy!
